Active Components

⚡

Diode

A semiconductor device that allows current to flow in one direction while blocking it in the opposite direction.
⚡

Transistor

Controls and amplifies electrical current in electronic circuits.
⚡

OCR

Maintains frequency stability by using a temperature-controlled oven to keep the crystal at a constant temperature.
⚡

Potentiometer

A variable resistor that adjusts voltage or current in a circuit by changing its resistance.
⚡

Resistor

Limits or regulates the flow of current in a circuit by providing resistance.
⚡

IC

Integrated circuits that combine multiple electronic components into a single package.
⚡

Capacitor

Stores electrical energy temporarily and releases it when needed in a circuit.
⚡

Transformer

Transfers electrical energy between circuits through inductively coupled conductors.
